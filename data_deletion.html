Request account deletion

If you want to delete your Eqly account and all associated data, choose one of the options below.

Option 1 — Self request by email

Send an email to support@yourdomain.com with your registered email and user id (if known). We will respond within 7 business days.

Option 2 — Submit a request (recommended)

We will verify the request and send a confirmation once deletion is complete. For security we may ask you to confirm via the registered email address. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
