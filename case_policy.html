Eqly – Child Sexual Abuse & Exploitation (CSAE) Safety Policy

Eqly maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any form of child exploitation. We are committed to ensuring our platform remains safe, lawful, and fully compliant with international child protection standards.

1. Prohibited Content

The following are strictly forbidden on Eqly:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexualized or exploitative content involving minors
  • Grooming, enticement, or attempts to contact minors for harmful purposes
  • Uploading, sharing, commenting, or distributing CSAM
  • AI-generated, edited, or fantasy content involving minors in sexual or exploitative contexts

2. Detection & Prevention

  • We use a combination of automated systems and manual review to identify harmful content.
  • Suspicious or harmful content is immediately removed.
  • Eqly does not allow minors to use the platform.

3. Enforcement Actions

Any user violating this policy will face:

  • Immediate account suspension or permanent ban
  • Removal of all related content
  • Reporting to law enforcement and cyber-tip organizations as required

4. Reporting

Users can report inappropriate content at any time using in-app reporting tools or by emailing support@eqly.in.

5. Cooperation With Authorities

Eqly fully cooperates with law enforcement agencies and responds promptly to valid legal requests as part of CSAE investigations.

6. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically to comply with global regulations, Google Play policies, and child safety standards.
